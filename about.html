Our Mission

To become the premier technology broker that brings great value and relevant innovation and technology to federal, state, and international participants.

Our Vision

  1. To contribute to the Charleston region and state of South Carolina innovation ecosystems to serve as a catalyst for regional industrial development by looking to the future, adapting to changing markets and delivering awareness that impacts both the public and private sectors based on:
    • Human Capital
    • Funding
    • Infrastructure
    • Demand
    • Culture Incentives
    • Applied Technology Innovation
  2. To serve as the collection point for external information on relevant organizational actions and practices, topics of interest, current affairs, and applications of policies related to create an ecosystem that serves national defense interest through shared knowledge and innovation.
  3. To bring together the thought leaders in government, industry, and academia to collaborate on cutting-edge organizational challenges facing our nation, region, state and local community. Representative group includes members with strategic national defense experience, engineering, and other scientific knowledge and innovation leaders.
